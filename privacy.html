Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

Grandma's Kitchen ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Name and email address (when you sign up for our newsletter or contact us)
  • Messages you send via our contact form

Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address, browser type, device type, and operating system
  • Pages viewed, time spent on pages, and referring URL
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To send recipes, newsletters, and respond to your inquiries
  • To improve site content, layout, and recipe selection
  • To analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • To display relevant advertising
  • To prevent fraud and protect site security

3. Cookies

We use cookies to remember your preferences and to understand how visitors use our site. You can choose to accept or decline cookies via our cookie banner. Most browsers also let you disable cookies through their settings, though some site features may not function properly without them.

4. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics (tracking ID: G-4J046JXWVC) to understand site traffic. Google Analytics uses cookies to collect anonymous information such as the number of visitors, where they came from, and which pages they viewed. You can opt out via the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

5. Google AdSense & Third-Party Advertising

We may participate in Google AdSense and other advertising programs. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our website and other sites on the Internet.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visits to our site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You may also opt out of third-party vendor cookie usage by visiting www.aboutads.info.

6. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data only with:

  • Service providers (e.g., email delivery, analytics) bound by confidentiality
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Successors in the event of a business transfer

7. Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your information
  • Opt out of marketing emails (use the unsubscribe link in any email)
  • Object to or restrict certain types of processing

To exercise these rights, email us at Grandparenting34@gmail.com.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, please contact us so we can delete it.

9. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
