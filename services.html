What We Cook Up

Recipes, videos, and a few extras for our community.

1. Easy Recipe Videos

Our flagship offering: slow-paced, easy-to-follow cooking videos focused on classic comfort food, casseroles, pot roasts, and "the old days" desserts. Every video uses big on-screen text and clear narration. Free to watch on our website.

2. Printable Recipe Cards

Every recipe on the site is also available as a free printable recipe card — large print, ingredients on the left, steps on the right. Stick it on the fridge or tuck it into your family recipe binder.

3. Sunday Newsletter

One supper idea every Sunday morning, free in your inbox. No spam, no clutter — just a recipe and a kind word.

4. Meal Plan Bundles

Looking for a week's worth of comfort food, planned out and shopping-list-ready? We publish seasonal bundles four times a year — perfect for busy households or families caring for someone who'd love a home-cooked meal.

5. Brand Partnerships

We work with a small number of brands each year — cookware, pantry staples, and family-friendly products that genuinely belong in a home kitchen. All sponsored content is clearly labeled. For partnership inquiries, email grandkitchen@gmail.com.

6. Community Recipe Exchange

Send us your family's most-loved recipe and we may feature it (with full credit) in a future video. The best recipes don't come from books — they come from grandmothers.

Our Promise

  • Every recipe tested at least three times.
  • Honest measurements — no shrunken portions.
  • Plain-English instructions, no jargon.
  • Sponsored content always disclosed.
